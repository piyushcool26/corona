Mutations of Corona

There could be many mutations in the fututre but to prevent them from happening we can do the following-
We should develop vaccines for earlier variants and vaccinate people as soon as possible.
Attention should be paid constantly for any difference in the symptoms and the body parts affected.
To minimize the impact of these mutations strict lockdown should be imposed in regions which are affected by the new variant
so as to protect other regions from getting these new variant cases.
