The Year In Which Coronavirus Pandemic Started

Here’s what we know about the virus that was first detected in Wuhan, China, in late 2019 and has set off a global pandemic. Experts say SARS-CoV-2 originated in bats. That’s also how the coronaviruses behind Middle East respiratory syndrome (MERS) and severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS) got started.
